Lorenz Attractor in p5

Drag to rotate, scroll to zoom.

The Lorenz Attractor is interesting because this simple system is 'attracted' to the same shape every time, even though it may take different paths to get there. It demonstrates how a physical system can be completely deterministic and yet still be inherently unpredictable¹.
