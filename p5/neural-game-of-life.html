Neural Game of Life in p5

Spacebar to reset the sketch. Mouse over the canvas to disturb the cell states.

This sketch reimagines Conway's Game of Life with continuous values. The update rule is expressed as a 3D function that takes the present cell state and the sum of neighbor states to the new cell state. Each quadrant of the sketch represents a different update function.
