Domain Warping in p5

Left click to reset the sketch.

Domain warping uses a nested noise function to create detailed textures. In this case, the noise coordinate is offset by a vector composed of the sin of two other noise functions.

This sketch excites me because it looks like satellite imagery of some desolate terrain, like a flyby of Titan in Jupiter's orbit.
